## Platform Overview

Affinity Echo is an anonymous-first professional networking platform built specifically for underrepresented communities in tech. The platform combines forums, mentorship, job referrals, and secure messaging into a unified experience that prioritizes authentic connections over superficial networking.

### Key Differentiators
- **Anonymous by Default**: All interactions start anonymously
- **Progressive Identity Revelation**: Users control when and to whom they reveal their identity
- **Affinity-Based Communities**: Built around shared experiences and backgrounds
- **Integrated Referral System**: Direct path from community to career opportunities
- **Privacy-First Architecture**: No tracking, no data selling, end-to-end encryption

### 4. Referral Marketplace

The Referral Marketplace connects job seekers with employees who can provide referrals, all while maintaining anonymity.

#### Two-Track System

**Track 1: Referral Requests (Job Seekers)**
- **Post Structure**:
  - Job title/role seeking
  - Target company(ies)
  - Job posting link (optional)
  - Background and qualifications
  - Why interested in this role/company
  - Skills and experience highlights
  - Tags for discoverability

**Track 2: Referral Offers (Employees)**
- **Post Structure**:
  - Company name
  - Roles they can refer for
  - Available slots (e.g., "3 referrals available")
  - Requirements/expectations
  - Referral process explanation
  - Response time commitment
  - Tags for discoverability

#### Filtering & Discovery
- **Type Filter**: Requests vs. Offers
- **Status Filter**: Open, In Progress, Closed
- **Scope Filter**: Global, Company-specific
- **Search**: Full-text search across all posts
- **Sort Options**:
  - Most Recent
  - Most Popular (engagement)
  - Most Relevant (to user's profile)
- **Company Filter**: Search by specific companies
- **Role Filter**: Filter by job function

#### Engagement Flow
1. **Browse Posts**: User finds relevant request/offer
2. **View Details**: Click to see full post with all information
3. **Send Connection Request**:
   - Compose message explaining interest
   - Request sent anonymously
   - Notification sent to post author
4. **Review Connection**: Post author reviews request
5. **Accept/Reject**: Author decides whether to connect
6. **Initial Anonymous Chat**: If accepted, users can message anonymously
7. **Identity Reveal Request**:
   - Either party can request identity reveal
   - Both must agree for identities to be revealed
8. **Full Identity**: Once revealed, users see real profiles and can continue networking

#### Post Management
- **Edit Posts**: Update information, close slots
- **Status Management**: Mark as open/closed
- **Connection Tracking**: See all pending/accepted requests
- **Success Metrics**: Track how many referrals led to interviews/offers

#### Referral Best Practices (Built-in)
- **Guidelines Display**: Show best practices when creating posts
- **Template Suggestions**: Pre-written templates for common scenarios
- **Quality Checks**: Prompts to improve post quality before publishing
- **Engagement Tips**: Advice on crafting compelling requests
